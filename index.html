This calculator helps you determine Stripe payment fees for international transactions.

  • Reverse Calculation: Enter the amount you want to receive to calculate the required principal
  • Forward Calculation: Enter the principal amount to calculate fees and final amount
  • Supports Visa, Mastercard (3% fee) and Amex (3.5% fee)
  • Includes 18% tax on processing fees (customizable)
  • Click on any result value to copy it
  • Customize fee percentages in settings
